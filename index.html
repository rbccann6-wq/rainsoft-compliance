SMS Messaging Program

Program Description

RainSoft of the Wiregrass operates an SMS text messaging program to communicate with existing customers and prospective customers who have opted in to receive messages. Messages are sent by RainSoft of the Wiregrass staff to customers and leads for the following purposes:

  • Appointment scheduling and reminders for free water tests and installations
  • Service follow-ups and customer satisfaction surveys
  • Financing information and application status updates
  • Job application status updates for employment candidates
  • Promotional offers and service announcements (for opted-in customers only)

How to Opt In

Customers and prospects may opt in to receive SMS messages from RainSoft of the Wiregrass through any of the following methods:

  • In-person: By providing a mobile phone number to a RainSoft representative during a water test appointment, in-home consultation, or Lowe's/Home Depot event and verbally consenting to receive texts
  • Web form: By submitting contact information through our website or a lead form and checking the SMS consent box
  • Text-in: By texting START or any keyword to our business number (334) 489-2782 or (334) 373-0429
  • Job applicants: By submitting a job application at our hiring portal and providing a mobile number, applicants consent to receive application status updates via SMS
Consent disclosure: By opting in to our SMS program, you agree to receive text messages from RainSoft of the Wiregrass at the phone number provided. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe at any time. Reply HELP for help. No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies by program. Appointment reminders are sent as needed. Promotional messages are sent no more than 4 times per month. Job application updates are sent as status changes occur.

How to Opt Out

You may opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any message from us
  • Calling us at (334) 308-2782
  • Emailing rebecca@rainsoftse.com

After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message. No further messages will be sent.

Help

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us directly at (334) 308-2782 or rebecca@rainsoftse.com.
